Inferō: The Decode

LLM inference, fully decoded.
by Chongming

Training a language model is the headline. Inference is the story. Every token generated, every millisecond of latency, every dollar of GPU time — that's the inference stack at work. A series for ML engineers and researchers who want to understand it fully: from KV caches and speculative decoding, to silicon and accelerators, to reasoning loops and agentic systems.
